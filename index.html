The first episode of Plush Kaisen.

🐰 Bonnie

Personality

Calm and determined, but can get frustrated when pushed too far. Bonnie adapts quickly and uses creativity during fights.

Cursed Technique

Silence of the Great

Bonnie pretends to hold a jar that absorbs background noise. The collected noise becomes cursed energy which is fired as blasts.

Domain Expansion

Noise Drowned Out by the Loud Ring of Silence

A domain where opponents experience powerful hallucinations while Bonnie controls the battlefield.

Fun Facts

  • Loves ramen.
  • Uses sound as a weapon.
  • One of the strongest fighters.

😈 Nightmare Bonnie

Personality

Aggressive, unpredictable, and driven by rage. Nightmare Bonnie fights through instinct and overwhelming power.

Cursed Technique

Cursed Energy Consumption

Nightmare Bonnie can consume cursed energy and grow stronger during battles.

Domain Expansion

Devouring Void

A domain focused on overwhelming opponents with his cursed energy.

Fun Facts

  • Is the reason his dad doesn't have a big toe.
  • Gets stronger during long fights.
  • His rage creates cursed energy.

🐊 Monty

Personality

Loud, confident, competitive, and loves fighting. Monty charges into problems instead of avoiding them.

Cursed Technique

Ruin Drive

A technique that increases strength and momentum, making attacks stronger as he keeps moving.

Domain Expansion

Crushing Pit

Fun Facts

  • Loves Pomni-chan.
  • Uses raw power over complicated strategies.
  • Loves testing his strength.

🦊 Foxy

Personality

Smart, confident, and tactical. Foxy usually thinks ahead before attacking.

Cursed Technique

AirPods Sync

Allows Foxy to coordinate teammates and communicate strategies.

Domain Expansion

Pirates Cove

Fun Facts

  • Stopped World War 3 at age 5.
  • Smarter than Einstein and Young Sheldon combined.
  • One of the group's main strategists.

🎭 Puppet

Personality

Creative, mysterious, and loves confusing opponents.

Cursed Technique

Prize Box Shikigami

A box shikigami technique that works similarly to Deadly Prize, creating traps and forcing opponents into its effect.

Domain Expansion

Uncanny Replica Theater

A domain filled with copies and illusions.

Fun Facts

  • Sent his dad's phone into the sun with his technique.
  • Uses clones and shikigami.
  • Wins through strategy.

🐻 Freddy

Personality

Energetic, dramatic, and loves making announcements.

Cursed Technique

Broadcast Control

Uses information and broadcasts as part of his abilities.

Domain Expansion

Breaking News Arena

Fun Facts

  • Is short.
  • Loves dramatic announcements.
  • Adds comedy to serious moments.

🐥 Chica

Personality

Chaotic, energetic, and unpredictable.

Cursed Technique

Chaotic Energy

Uses unpredictable cursed energy patterns.

Domain Expansion

Golden Chaos

Fun Facts

  • Secretly a huge weeb.
  • Her favorite anime is Death Note.
  • Changes fighting style constantly.
