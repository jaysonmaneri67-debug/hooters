🐰 Bonnie

Personality

Bonnie is calm, creative, and determined. He can become frustrated when pushed too far, but adapts quickly during fights.

Cursed Technique

Silence of the Great

Bonnie pretends to hold a jar that absorbs background noise. The collected sound becomes cursed energy that is fired as blasts.

Domain Expansion

Noise Drowned Out by the Loud Ring of Silence

A domain where everyone experiences heavy hallucinations while Bonnie controls the battlefield.

Fun Facts

  • Loves ramen.
  • Uses sound as a weapon.
  • One of the strongest fighters.

😈 Nightmare Bonnie

Personality

Aggressive, unpredictable, and powered by rage.

Cursed Technique

Cursed Energy Consumption

Nightmare Bonnie can consume cursed energy and grow stronger during battles.

Domain Expansion

Devouring Void

Fun Facts

  • The reason his dad doesn't have a big toe.
  • His rage creates cursed energy.
  • Gets stronger as fights continue.
