🐰 Bonnie

Personality

Calm but can become frustrated when pushed too far. A determined fighter who adapts to situations.

Cursed Technique

Silence of the Great
Bonnie creates a fake jar that absorbs background noise and converts it into cursed energy blasts.

Domain Expansion

Noise Drowned Out by the Loud Ring of Silence
A domain where opponents experience intense hallucinations while Bonnie controls the battlefield.

Fun Facts

  • Loves ramen.
  • Uses sound as a weapon.
  • One of the strongest fighters.

😈 Nightmare Bonnie

Personality

Aggressive, unpredictable, and driven by rage.

Cursed Technique

Cursed Energy Consumption
Consumes cursed energy and grows stronger.

Domain Expansion

Devouring Void

Fun Facts

  • The reason his dad doesn't have a big toe.
  • Gets stronger during long battles.
  • His rage creates cursed energy.

🐊 Monty

Personality

Loud, confident, and loves fighting.

Cursed Technique

Ruin Drive
Increases strength through momentum.

Domain Expansion

Crushing Pit

Fun Facts

  • Loves Pomni-chan.
  • Uses overwhelming power.
  • Enjoys testing his strength.

🦊 Foxy

Personality

Strategic, confident, and usually the one planning ahead. Foxy prefers using intelligence and teamwork over rushing in.

Cursed Technique

AirPods Sync
Foxy uses communication and coordination to organize the team's attacks.

Domain Expansion

Pirates Cove
A battlefield controlled by Foxy's strategies.

Fun Facts

  • Stopped World War 3 at age 5.
  • Smarter than Einstein and Young Sheldon combined.
  • One of the main strategists of the group.

🎭 Puppet

Personality

Creative, mysterious, and unpredictable. Puppet prefers confusing enemies with tricks.

Cursed Technique

Prize Box Shikigami
A box shikigami technique that works similarly to Deadly Prize. It creates a trap effect that forces opponents into its ability.

Domain Expansion

Uncanny Replica Theater
A domain filled with copies and illusions.

Fun Facts

  • Sent his dad's phone into the sun using his technique.
  • Uses clones and shikigami.
  • Wins through strategy instead of raw strength.

🐻 Freddy

Personality

Energetic, dramatic, and loves making announcements.

Cursed Technique

Broadcast Control
Uses information and broadcasts as part of his abilities.

Domain Expansion

Breaking News Arena
A domain based around broadcasts and information.

Fun Facts

  • Is short.
  • Loves dramatic announcements.
  • Adds comedy to serious moments.

🐥 Chica

Personality

Cursed Technique

Domain Expansion

Golden Chaos

Fun Facts

  • Secretly a huge weeb.

  • Her fighting style changes constantly.
