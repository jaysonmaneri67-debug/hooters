🐰 Bonnie

Personality

Bonnie is usually calm and collected, but can become frustrated when pushed too far. He is a determined fighter who relies on adapting to situations and using his environment.

Role

Special Grade level fighter with a unique cursed technique focused on sound and cursed energy conversion.

Cursed Technique

Silence of the Great
Bonnie creates the appearance of holding a jar that absorbs background noise. The collected sound is transformed into cursed energy which can be released as powerful blasts.

Domain Expansion

Noise Drowned Out by the Loud Ring of Silence
Inside the domain, sound and perception become heavily distorted. Opponents experience intense hallucinations while Bonnie controls the battlefield.

Fun Facts

  • Uses a Gojo/Yuji inspired fighting style.
  • One of the strongest fighters in the group.
  • Turns something normally harmless (noise) into a weapon.

😈 Nightmare Bonnie

Personality

Nightmare Bonnie is aggressive, unpredictable, and driven by rage. Unlike normal Bonnie, he relies more on overwhelming force and instinct.

Role

A terrifying special-grade threat with cursed energy that grows during battle.

Cursed Technique

Cursed Energy Consumption
Nightmare Bonnie can bite and consume cursed energy from the air and opponents, allowing him to grow stronger during fights.

Domain Expansion

Devouring Void
A domain where opponents are trapped against a constantly growing cursed-energy monster.

Fun Facts

  • He is the opposite of Bonnie's calmer personality.
  • His power increases the longer a battle lasts.
  • His rage itself creates cursed energy.

🐊 Monty

Personality

Monty is loud, confident, and loves fighting. He has a wild personality and charges into battles head-first.

Role

Power-based fighter specializing in raw strength.

Cursed Technique

Ruin Drive
Monty increases his power through momentum, making every attack stronger as he continues fighting.

Domain Expansion

Crushing Pit
A domain designed to trap opponents in a battlefield where Monty's physical power dominates.

Fun Facts

  • Loves golf.
  • One of the strongest close-range fighters.
  • Uses brute force instead of complicated strategies.

🦊 Foxy

Personality

Foxy is strategic, confident, and acts as the planner of the group. He prefers thinking before attacking.

Role

Grade 1 strategist and team coordinator.

Cursed Technique

AirPods Sync
Foxy uses communication and coordination to organize his team's movements.

Domain Expansion

Pirates Cove
A battlefield controlled by Foxy's strategies and pirate-themed attacks.

Fun Facts

  • Usually coordinates the team.
  • Uses intelligence instead of raw power.
  • Known for planning fights.

🎭 Puppet

Personality

Puppet is mysterious, creative, and unpredictable. They prefer confusing enemies rather than overpowering them directly.

Role

A technique-based fighter who specializes in clones and shikigami abilities.

Cursed Technique

Prize Box Shikigami
Puppet summons a box shikigami that works similarly to Bonnie's Deadly Prize concept. The ability creates a trap that influences opponents and pulls them into the box's effect.

Domain Expansion

Uncanny Replica Theater
A domain filled with copies and illusions that make it difficult for enemies to know what is real.

Fun Facts

  • Uses strategy and deception.
  • Can overwhelm enemies through confusion.
  • His abilities focus on copies and traps.

🐻 Freddy

Personality

Freddy is energetic, dramatic, and loves making announcements. He often brings humor into serious situations.

Role

The group's reporter and information source.

Cursed Technique

Broadcast Control
Freddy uses information and broadcasts as part of his abilities.

Domain Expansion

Breaking News Arena
A domain where previous broadcasts and information become part of the battlefield.

Fun Facts

  • Known for dramatic announcements.
  • Often interrupts serious moments.
  • Adds comedy to the team.

🐥 Chica

Personality

Chica is chaotic, energetic, and difficult to predict. Her fighting style changes depending on the situation.

Role

A wildcard fighter.

Cursed Technique

Chaotic Energy
Uses unpredictable cursed energy patterns to confuse opponents.

Domain Expansion

Golden Chaos
A domain where unpredictability becomes Chica's advantage.

Fun Facts

  • One of the most unpredictable fighters.
  • Creates chaos during battles.
  • Never fights the same way twice.
